Welcome to Impromptu Café, KU’s very own award-winning full service restaurant. Conveniently located on level 3 of the Kansas Union, our mission is to offer students, visitors, staff and faculty a fresh and seasonal lunch experience in a casual, friendly atmosphere. Don’t let the cloth napkins fool you; we operate on a first-come, first-served basis and our prices are very reasonable.
Pressed for time? No problem – just call us at 864-8001 for quick, convenient carry-out.
Out of cash? We welcome Beak ‘Em Bucks and KU Cuisine Cash!
Today’s Hours11:00 am - 2:00 pm : all hours
Hours: Impromptu is generally open M-F, 11am &#8211; 2pm, when classes are in session, as well as Stop Day and Finals Week. The cafe is closed for Spring Break, Labor Day, Fall Break, Thanksgiving Break and Winter Break.  view all KU Dining hours.
&nbsp;
Join us and treat yourself to Executive Chef Janna Traver’s tried-and -true menu favorites, or ask your student server about seasonal selections and our always-popular daily specials.  Click the menu image to the left to view our full menu.
&nbsp;
Personalized, flexible service is our trademark.
We offer a wide range of options for our eclectic clientele; much of our menu is vegetarian or can be prepared as such, and we offer many vegan choices. We are happy to guide our guests with dietary restrictions, allergies, or sensitivities to appropriate and delicious menu choices.
Sustainability matters. As part of the overall KU Dining Services sustainability initiative, we are committed to supporting local producers and farmers, and we work to keep our carbon footprint small. Fresh herbs and tomatoes from Chef Janna’s Union rooftop garden inspire our specials during the growing season, and we strive to use locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible.
Large groups are welcome in our private dining alcove, perfect for lunch meetings or retreats. For more information, or to reserve the Impromptu alcove for your group of 8 to 20 people, please contact kimnixon@ku.edu or call 864-8001.
We offer direct billing to KU departments.This service allows you to entertain colleagues, visitors, and prospective candidates without the hassle of carrying cash. If your department would like to utilize this service, please fill out this form this form and remit to kimnixon@ku.edu.
