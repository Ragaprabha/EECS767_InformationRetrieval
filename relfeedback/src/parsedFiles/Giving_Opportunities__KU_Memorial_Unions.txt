Operational Support
Kansas Union Programming
Provides project support for planned improvements of the Union operations. This includes renovations, capital purchases of equipment and amenities.
acct. no. 37166
KU History and Traditions
The Unions love to share KU History, traditions and the accomplishments of its faculty, staff and students. Initiatives currently include KUHistory.com, historical galleries, displays, public lectures and social media. As the gathering place of KU, these initiatives not only entertain and educate, they serve as an important recruiting tool for visitors to campus.
acct. no. 40817
Operational Support for Student Groups and Programs
The Big Event Program
The Big Event connects the KU campus with the Lawrence community by recruiting thousands of student, faculty, and staff volunteers to work at hundreds of local job sites during one day of service. Big Event volunteers provide a helping hand to Lawrence residents by completing projects in their neighborhoods.
acct. no. 40816
KJHK-FM Student-Run Radio
Benefits KJHK, the student-run radio station broadcasting from the Kansas Union. Funds may be used to improve the station in many ways. These include the purchase of equipment, staff education, travel for educational purposes, to enhance news or sports coverage or attend recognition ceremonies.

Frank Burge Scholarship
Scholarships for students actively engaged in leadership roles in Student Union Activities. Must display outstanding character and be in good academic standing with a 3.0 GPA or above. Recipient should have unquestionable integrity who has made significant contributions to the goals of SUA.
