More Resources&nbsp;    
Commuting by bicycle is a great way to get around campus and the Lawrence community. By riding your bike you will live a healthier lifestyle, reduce your environmental impact, and save on transportation costs. But more than that, you'll have a lot of fun doing it!
This site was created to help you make the most of your cycling experience, so please check out the tabs on the left to learn more about biking on KU's campus and in the the Lawrence community.

Bike Stand 
The Student Recreation Fitness Center has an all inclusive bike reapair stand for student, faculty, and staff use! It is located by the outdoor basketball courts on the south side of the building.

              Bike Rental Program
      The Outdoor Pursuits program at the Student Recreation Fitness Center offers a Bike Rental program. There are 12  bikes ready to rent: 8 mountain bikes and 4 comfort bikes. There are daily, weekend, and weekly rates available.
Comfort Bike Model: Giant Sedona DX
Mountain Bike Model: Giant Revel 1
Contact Outdoor Pursuits at outdoorpursuits@ku.edu or (785) 864-1843 for additional questions and/or inquires or check out the FAQ document.
