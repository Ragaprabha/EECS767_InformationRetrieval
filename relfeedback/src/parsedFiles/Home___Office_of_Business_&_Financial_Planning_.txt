Office of Business &amp; Financial Planning
The Office of Business &amp; Financial Planning is responsible for a variety of university wide financial matters and is the liaison to a number of affiliated corporations, the Kansas Board of Regents», and other state agencies. The Office is responsible for all aspects of bond financing for the University including reviewing and approving all debt financing, overseeing compliance with bond covenants, and monitoring bond ratios and debt capacity. We work with KU Endowment» staff on real estate, property, and lease related issues and coordinates campus real estate issues. The Office of Risk Management and the University Aviation Services Department also are part of the Office of Business &amp; Financial Planning.
