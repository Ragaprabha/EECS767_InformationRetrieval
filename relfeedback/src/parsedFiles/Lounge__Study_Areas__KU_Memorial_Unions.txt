	Main Lounge Area, Level 4:
Located next to Roasterie Coffeehouse, this space is in located in the heart of the Kansas Union.  With plenty of soft seating as well as tables and chairs, this area is great for meeting up with friends and people watching over a cup of coffee or fruit smoothie.  It&#8217;s also where students can enjoy SUA&#8217;s Tea at Three, offered every Thursday at 3:00pm&#8211;free hot tea and cookies!
	Traditions Lounge, Level 4:
A piano that any student can enjoy and plenty of comfortable seating, this space is a student favorite. Out of the fray of the Main Lounge Area, this space offers study space and great spaces for book clubs or study groups to meet.  It&#8217;s also home to KU Basketball Away Game Watch Parties, where students can enjoy the game on a 132&#8243; screen, with free popcorn and Coke beverages!

This is home to one of the very best views on campus, overlooking Memorial Stadium. A quite place to read, study or enjoy a quiet lunch.
