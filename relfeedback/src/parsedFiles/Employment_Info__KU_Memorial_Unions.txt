Great Food. Great People. If you´re looking for a job that´s close to where you live and offers good pay and a flexible schedule, look no further. KU Dining Services is one of the largest employers of students on campus, and with good reason!
There are approximately 200 full-time positions within the Unions offering benefits including health insurance, retirement benefits and paid vacation and sick leave. We also employ hundreds of people in temporary positions each year. Join us in serving the University of Kansas!

Why Join the KU Dining Team?
	A starting hourly (student) rate of $9.22* an hour for retail and residential employees. (*catering rates are $9.22 an hour)
	Discounted meals for shifts worked
	Annual academic scholarship opportunities available
	A huge variety of positions to suit your unique talents, including clerical support, baristas, cashiers, food prep, line service, custodial, restaurant servers and catering attendants, as well as student supervisor/crew leader positions
Check here for both full-time and temporary positions available in the Unions.
