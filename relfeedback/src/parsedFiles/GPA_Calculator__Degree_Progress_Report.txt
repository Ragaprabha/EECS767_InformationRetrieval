How To Use The Semester/Cumulative GPA Calculator:
Semester GPA
	Enter the credit hours for each course that you are enrolled in.
	Select the grade you earned, or think you will earn, in each course.
	Next to the semester GPA box at the bottom, press the "Calculate" button.
Cumulative GPA
	Enter the KU cumulative GPA you have earned so far.
	Enter the total KU A-F hours that you have earned so far.
	Enter the number of credit hours for each class that you are currently enrolled in.
	Select the grade you earned or think you will earn in each course.
	Next to the cumulative GPA box at the bottom, press the "Calculate" button.
