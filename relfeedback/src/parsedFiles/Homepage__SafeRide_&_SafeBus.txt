        SafeRide
SafeRide operates 10:30 p.m. - 2:30 a.m. seven nights per week, except during class breaks and holidays.&nbsp; Call 785-864-SAFE [7233] during those hours to request a ride.
SafeBus
SafeBus operates 9 p.m. - 3 a.m. Thursday/Friday/Saturday nights,&nbsp;except during class breaks and holidays.
            Fast Facts
                                    Apply &raquo;
                                            We gave 78,981 safe rides home in 2012 
                                            SafeRide & SafeBus are 100% funded by student fees
                                            SafeBus operates 9 p.m.– 3 a.m. Thursday through Saturday during fall and spring semesters
                                            SafeRide operates 7 nights a week during fall and spring semesters
                                            SafeRide operates 10:30 p.m.–2:30 a.m. Thursday through Saturday during the summer semester
                                            SafeRide only takes you home, not to someone else’s home
                                            Call 864-SAFE between 10:30 p.m. and 2:30 a.m. to get a ride
                                            No drinks are allowed in SafeBus or SafeRide vehicles
                                            Please don’t distract the driver while on board
                                            Visibility around buses is extremely limited; don’t run after a bus once it pulls away
