Gallery Guidelines&nbsp;    
                                Art &amp; Design Gallery
    Next  
Located on the third floor of Chalmers Hall, the Art &amp; Design Gallery serves as&nbsp;a teaching space. Students from the department, as well as from the university at large, are exposed to art by their peers, visiting and regional artists, and faculty members.&nbsp;Many Visual Art students experience installing and exhibiting their work in a gallery setting for the first time in this space.&nbsp;The 2,100 square foot gallery has been recently renovated. The gallery is free and open to the public.
&nbsp;

8:30am – 4:30pm
